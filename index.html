Neta Singer

Hello! I am a third year PhD student at École Polytechnique Fédéral de Lausanne (EPFL) in the Discrete Optimization Lab. I graduated Cum Laude with a B.A. in Mathematics from Barnard College, Columbia University. I completed my M.S. in Mathematics from EPFL in 2023.

I am broadly interested in combinatorial optimization, approximation algorithms, and integer programming.

I do lots of dancing in my free time and I trained at Kibbutz Contemporary Dance Company and at Barnard College. For some memorable dance moments, check out the image gallery or my dance reel.
